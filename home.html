ABOUT FOREVER CHEMICALS

Forever chemicals, scientifically known as per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS), are synthetic compounds that have been widely used in various industries for their resistance to heat, water, and grease. These chemicals are found in everyday products such as non-stick cookware, water-repellent fabrics, and food packaging.

we're exploring innovative solutions to break down and recover fluorine from these chemicals, turning a pollution problem into a valuable resource for industries like aluminum production.

Through advanced recovery processes, we aim to extract fluorine ions from PFAS and reuse them, reducing both environmental contamination and the need for mining fresh materials.
